Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 4, 2025

What We Collect

LogSentry is designed with privacy as a priority. We only collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your email address when you register for an account. We do not collect passwords; all authentication is handled securely by Firebase Authentication.
  • Usage Data (Guests): For non-registered users, we store a simple, anonymous counter in your browser's session to enforce the "3 free uses" trial. This counter is not linked to any personal information.

What We Do NOT Collect

We do not store, log, or review the contents of the log files you upload.

All file processing and AI analysis is done in-memory. The log file you upload is deleted from our server immediately after the analysis is complete. The AI-generated solutions are also not stored.

How We Use Your Information

We use your email address *only* to provide you with access to your account and to provide unlimited access to the Service's features.
