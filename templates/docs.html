What is LogSentry?

LogSentry is an intelligent log analysis tool designed to help developers, SREs, and operations teams quickly diagnose and solve application errors. Instead of manually searching through thousands of lines, you can upload your log, and LogSentry will automatically identify and extract critical errors.

Its most powerful feature is the built-in AI assistant. With one click, you can get an expert-level explanation and a step-by-step solution for any detected error, dramatically reducing your Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR).

Supported Log Formats

LogSentry is designed for maximum flexibility. It works best with any text-based log file (`.log`, `.txt`) that uses a common "level-based" format.

Primary Format (CSV-style)

The parser is optimized for logs where each line is a record, and one of the fields (usually the second) is the log level. For example:

10/26/2025 10:07:25 PM, Information, ...message...
10/26/2025 10:17:28 PM, Warning, ...message...
10/26/2025 10:27:30 PM, Error, ...message...
Other Formats

The parser will still attempt to find lines that begin with `Error,`, `Warning,`, or `Information,` even if the timestamp is missing.

Security & Privacy

Your data is never stored. LogSentry processes files entirely in memory. Your log file is read, analyzed, and then immediately deleted from the server. We do not keep or review any user log data.

About the AI

The "Analyze with AI" feature is available for registered users. It requires a server-side API key (set by the site administrator) to function. Your log data is sent securely to the AI provider only when you click the "Analyze" button and is not stored or logged by us.
